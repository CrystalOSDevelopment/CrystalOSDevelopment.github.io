About CrystalOS

CrystalOS is an open-source cosmos-based operating system with multiple functionalities.
Since the first builds released in early 2021 it gained popularity reaching almost 60 thousand views on youtube in total.
3 years after the start of the project CrystalOS is near the pre-release phase! with ♥ by Crystal Development
